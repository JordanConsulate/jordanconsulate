Visa Application Process

1

Determine Visa Type

Choose the appropriate visa category based on your purpose of visit to Jordan.

2

Gather Required Documents

Collect all necessary documents as per the requirements of your chosen visa type.

3

Complete Application Form

Fill out the visa application form accurately and completely.

4

Pay Visa Fees

Submit the required visa fees as per the current fee structure.

5

Submit Application

Submit your completed application along with all required documents to the Jordanian Consulate.

6

Visa Processing

Wait for the processing of your visa application. Processing times may vary depending on the visa type.

7

Collect Your Visa

Once approved, collect your visa from the Jordanian Consulate or as directed.

Visa Fees

Please note that visa fees are subject to change. The following table provides an overview of the current fee structure:

Visa Type Single Entry Multiple Entry Processing Time
Tourist Visa $40 $100 3-5 working days
Business Visa $60 $150 5-7 working days
